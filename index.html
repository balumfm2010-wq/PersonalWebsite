About Me

Bridging Finance & Digital Automation

I specialise in closing the gap between traditional finance operations and high-efficiency digital systems — with Blackline implementation and process optimisation at the core of everything I do.

My career began in the trenches of India's most respected tech giants — Infosys, TCS, and Capgemini — where I cut my teeth on large-scale financial operations, ERP integrations, and multinational reporting frameworks. Each engagement deepened my conviction: finance teams deserve smarter tools, not more spreadsheets.

Today, as part of the Lennox International team, I lead end-to-end implementations of Blackline's full module suite, drive digital COE initiatives, and champion a culture where automation frees finance professionals to do their highest-value work.
